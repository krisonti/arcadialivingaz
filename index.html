The Location

Arcadia, Phoenix

4334 E Indianola Ave, Phoenix, AZ 85018

Arcadia is consistently ranked among the most sought-after neighborhoods in the Valley — a rare, lushly green pocket between Phoenix and Scottsdale, framed by Camelback Mountain and fed by a century-old canal irrigation system that turned old citrus farmland into some of the greenest streets in the desert. That same irrigation is what keeps this lot's lawn green without the water bill.

From here it's roughly 15 minutes to Biltmore Fashion Park, Scottsdale Fashion Square, Old Town Scottsdale, and Sky Harbor Airport — central enough for an easy commute, quiet enough to feel tucked away. Much of the area feeds into Scottsdale Unified School District, including Arcadia High School, though boundaries vary block to block and should be verified directly for this address.

Limited land and steady demand have made Arcadia one of Phoenix's most consistently appreciating neighborhoods — new construction on a lot this size is genuinely uncommon this close in.
